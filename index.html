Privacy Policy – Rozsetu

Rozsetu respects the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information.

Information We Collect

  • Google account information such as email address and user ID for authentication
  • Task-related submissions such as screenshots or other proof
  • UPI ID provided by the user for reward payouts
  • Basic device and app usage information

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To process and review task submissions
  • To approve tasks and provide reward payouts
  • To prevent fraud and misuse of the platform
  • To improve app functionality and customer support

Data Sharing

Rozsetu does not sell or rent personal user data. Data may be shared only when required for task verification, legal compliance, fraud prevention, or as required by law.

Data Security

We use Firebase services and industry-standard security measures to protect user data. All data is encrypted in transit.

Data Retention

User data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, complete task verification, comply with legal obligations, and prevent fraud.

Account and Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their Rozsetu account and associated personal data at any time by sending an email request to rozsetu@gmail.com from their registered Google account email.

The following data will be permanently deleted after verification:

  • User account and authentication identifiers
  • Submitted task proofs and screenshots
  • UPI ID provided for reward payouts
  • Associated personal information

Certain data may be retained temporarily if required for legal, regulatory, fraud-prevention, or compliance purposes. Such data will be deleted once the applicable retention period ends.

Children’s Privacy

Rozsetu is intended only for users aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
